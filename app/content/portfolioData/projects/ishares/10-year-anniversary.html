Simple solutions are not always easy, but there is always a way. It takes years of experience to find simple solutions in complex problems.

Request:
Create an automated / interactive timeline for the iShares 10 year anniversary.

Solution:
Year over year, there is an underlying report on iShares AUM (Assets Under Management). As the time line transitions through each year, the AUM moves along the line and brings the user view with it. As each year comes into view, the events from that year are displayed to show the progress of the company.

Technologies:
Flash AS3, MVC architecture. XML data for each year. Procedural motion design.
